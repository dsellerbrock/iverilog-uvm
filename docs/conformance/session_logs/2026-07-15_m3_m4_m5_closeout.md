### Harness lesson: "(no-check)" can mask a non-running test

The first M4+M5 UVM sweep reported 136/136 but vif_smoke_v2 scored
"PASS (no-check)": the M4b object-outer store emitted
`%aa/viv/o/obj` for CLASS-KEYED assoc outers (the
uvm_pool#(uvm_object,...) chained-store shape) and that vivify form
did not exist — vvp rejected the whole program with "Invalid
opcode", producing neither a PASS nor a FAIL pattern, which the
harness counts as a pass. Fixed by adding %aa/viv/{o,sig}/obj over
the existing object-key assoc machinery (commit 8a5b0b5); the sweep
was re-captured with per-test output and audited for no-check
results. Follow-up recorded: the harness should treat "Invalid
opcode"/"Program not runnable" as FAIL patterns.

An M4-group UVM sweep that reported 135/135 mid-session was also
invalidated (binaries reinstalled mid-run) and repeated.
